The Challengers

Royal Challengers Bangalore is one of the most passionate franchises in the Indian Premier League. Based in the cricket-crazy city of Bangalore, RCB has built a legacy of thrilling, aggressive cricket that captures fans worldwide.

Known for their explosive batting lineups, fiery atmosphere at the M. Chinnaswamy Stadium, and the never-give-up spirit — RCB embodies the heart of Indian cricket fandom. After years of near-misses, the franchise claimed its first IPL title in 2024.
